About Us

Our mission is to empower young Students to be the inventors and creators.

        In a world where so much is being done for technology and so little for the environment, education is not even a part of most discussions. We at Studytonight believe that by widening the reach of education, by making it freely available, so much can be achieved.
        And this journey started when a thought come "wouldn't it be great, to have a website, with simple tutorials for programming languages, just like a friend would teach you!", and Coderscope was born.

        We at Coderscope deeply cherish your views regarding our website's and mobile app's performance and features. Whether it is doing it's job well, up to the mark or marvellously.
         And for this we require your involvement, for any type of suggestion, question, complaint or something that you think is missing in our products, or you have any new idea, please feel free to contact us.
